Manual AWS Audit Review vs Blackbox Auditor

Reducing audit cost while improving evidence quality. External auditors are under sustained cost pressure. Blackbox Auditor exists to reverse that equation.

The Cost Reality of Manual AWS Reviews

Budgets are tighter. Fee compression is real. Review expectations are increasing.

Manual AWS access and scope reviews typically involve:

  • Exporting data from multiple AWS services
  • Interpreting complex IAM and trust relationships
  • Manually validating scope and exposure
  • Reworking evidence after review comments

This work is usually performed by senior staff because junior auditors lack the AWS depth required.

That model does not scale.
