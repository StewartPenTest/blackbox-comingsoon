What Sample Reports Will Include

IAM Audit Evidence

User/role/privilege summaries, access key analysis, MFA status, and root user evidence. See how complex IAM data becomes auditor-ready tables.

SSO & Federated Access

Federated group to role mappings, identity provider detection, and effective permissions through federation.

External Boundary & Scope

Internet-facing services, public endpoints, and exposure by service type. Validate audit scope with actual discovery data.

Evidence Format

All sample reports demonstrate the same format used in production:

  • Normalized, human-readable tables — No JSON interpretation required
  • Timestamped outputs — Clear evidence of when data was collected
  • Risk indicators — Elevated and administrative access clearly flagged
  • Audit-aligned structure — Designed for walkthroughs, sampling, and re-performance
  • Reproducible evidence — Same inputs produce same outputs
